Discover our rich history, dedicated teachers, and commitment to excellence in education for Grade 11 & 12 students.

1995

A Legacy of Excellence

Everest Youth Academy was founded in 1995 with a simple mission: to provide quality education that prepares students for success in national exams and beyond. What started as a small tutoring center has grown into one of Addis Ababa's most respected secondary schools.

Today, we serve over 500 Grade 11 and 12 students with a dedicated team of 30+ teachers who are experts in their subjects. Our focus on exam preparation has resulted in a 95% pass rate for national exams over the past decade.
